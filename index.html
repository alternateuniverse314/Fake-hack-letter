📧 Create Your Prank Letter

How It Works:

Create a custom URL that displays a fake letter. When someone opens it, they'll get a surprise!

Step 1: Add a ?text= parameter to the URL with your custom message
https://alternateuniverse314.github.io/Fake-hack-letter/?text=You Won A Prize!
Step 2: Share the link with someone
Step 3: When they click it, they see your custom message as a letter subject

Example:

URL:
https://alternateuniverse314.github.io/Fake-hack-letter/?text=You Won A Prize!

Result: Shows a letter with the subject "You Won A Prize!" and an "Open Letter" button that the repient will think will open the message/letter but it reveals the prank.

More Examples:

  • ?text=Your account has been compromised
  • ?text=Urgent: Security alert
  • ?text=Click if you dare!
  • ?text=Let's play a game!

Have fun pranking responsibly!
